story

From kitchen to keyboard

    My name is Michal and I am based in Bristol. For the last 13 years I was working as a Professional Chef.
Cooking is one of my greatest passions, but now, I am ready to put a step forward in my professional life and change my career path.
Last year, during a pandemic, the whole hospitality sector experienced great difficulties and I observed lots of my colleagues in trade struggling with uncertainty of tomorrow.
I realised the need to change the industry I worked for. Taking motivation from my family and being driven by need to grow and develop, I decided to focus on the future and started to learn web development technologies and programing. Since then, I'm exploring new things everyday and improving skills I’ve already acquired.
I have a passion to code, despite the fact that I only do it for a few months, I feel like it comes naturally to me. Of course, I am only at the beginning of the this journey, but it quickly became a part of me. It surely affected the way I think and see things now. Completing a project, making simple applications or thinking about big ones gives me great satisfaction and motivates me in my learning process.
I’m 35, not the youngest, yet I am proud that in this age I have made an attempt to change my profession and leave my comfort zone.
I will try to update my projects as they come to give you an insight of what I’m doing. If you want to contact me about coding or cooking, please do! I am excited at the prospect of future projects and all the possibilities!
Cheers!
